01 / About

Hardware roots.
Software soul.

I am an Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering student who sees technology not just as circuits and signals, but as a canvas for innovation. While my academic roots lie in understanding the logic of hardware, my curiosity naturally evolved toward crafting digital experiences that people can interact with, feel, and remember.

I specialize in building seamless and responsive web interfaces using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while also developing robust backend systems with Java. For me, coding is not just about functionality — it's about creating harmony between performance, design, and user experience. I enjoy transforming ideas into structured, scalable solutions that reflect both precision and creativity.

What sets me apart is my ability to bridge two worlds: the analytical discipline of engineering and the imaginative freedom of design. This intersection drives me to think beyond conventional boundaries and build solutions that are not only technically sound but also visually compelling.

I am continuously learning, experimenting, and pushing my limits to grow into a versatile full-stack developer. My goal is to create meaningful digital products that solve real-world problems while delivering intuitive and engaging user experiences.
